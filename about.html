ABOUT SUNSURE

Your trusted Renewable Energy partner

Sunsure is not just a renewable energy company, we're your trusted partner in achieving sustainable growth. We are a leading Independent Power Producer (IPP) specializing in long-term Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs) that empower businesses across India to embrace clean energy with ease and confidence.
Born from a vision for a greener tomorrow, Sunsure was established with the unwavering belief that renewable energy holds the key to India's bright future. We are backed by the global investment expertise of Partners Group, a testament to our commitment to innovation and stability.

OUR Vision
We aim to power the world with clean renewable energy.
OUR MISSION
Be the driving force behind India's clean energy transformation, delivering reliable and accessible renewable power to every corner of the nation, by architecting high-performing renewable energy solutions for every industry.

Know More

Partners Group Holding AG is a Swiss-based global private equity firm with US$135 billion in assets under management in private equity, private infrastructure, private real estate and private debt. The firm manages a broad range of funds, structured products and customized portfolios for an international clientele of institutional investors, private banks and other financial institutions. The firm has completed more than 250 private equity investments in portfolio companies.

As end of 2021, Partners Group is the fifth most-valuable publicly listed private markets firm in the world by market capitalization. Sunsure Energy raised a round of $400 million from Partners Group AG to create renewable energy assets across India. The funding would enable Sunsure to reach its goal of installing 3GW of production capacity in 5 years.

Manoj Kohli

Former Country Head - SoftBank India, Former CEO – Airtel, DCM Shriram, Escorts.

Mr. Manoj Kohli, a seasoned entrepreneur, served as the Country Head of SoftBank India till January 2023, overseeing a $15 billion investment in 25 tech-focused portfolio companies such as Flipkart, Ola, Lenskart and Delhivery. Prior to this, he was the Managing Director and CEO of Bharti Airtel, leading its transformation into the third-largest global telco with a unique business model and extensive operations. Airtel was adjudged the 'Company of the Year' by the Economic Times under his leadership.

Mr. Kohli's leadership also extended to SB Energy, achieving over 5GW of renewable energy capacity. Recognized for his contributions, he received awards such as "Telecom Man of the Year" and holds degrees in Commerce, Law, and an MBA. Passionate about fitness, Mr. Kohli emphasises the importance of a healthy and joyful life.
