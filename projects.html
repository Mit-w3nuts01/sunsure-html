Commissioned Projects

Our plants in India

Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Ltd. – 17.5 MWp

The 17.5 MWp Ground-mounted Solar Power Plant is installed within the premises of the Kapilas Cement Works factory of Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Ltd. This plant is one of the largest behind-the-meter plants in India at the time of its commissioning and was completed by Sunsure within record construction timelines of 60 days from start of construction at the site. This is also DCBL's largest captive solar power plant till date.
