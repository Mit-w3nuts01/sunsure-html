RE 100 Solutions

Sunsure offers a range of renewable energy solutions, including solar power solutions, long-term power purchase agreements (PPAs), and utility-scale and distributed renewable energy projects.

  • RTC Power
  • Energy Storage

Green Energy Open Access

Sunsure Energy offers Open Access Solar plants. These plants can provide over 70% of a large industrial power demand. The power is delivered through the existing grid connection, and any excess power can be saved for later use.

  • Hybrid PPAs
  • Solar PPAs
  • Wind PPAs

Green Energy On-Site

Sunsure Energy offers long-term power purchase agreements (PPAs) for on-site and off-site renewable energy power plants. These PPAs allow industries to offset more than 70% of their power usage with renewable energy technologies like wind, solar, and battery storage.

  • Ground Mounted
